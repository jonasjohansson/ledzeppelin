# Devices & scanning

> **Status: outline — draft in progress.** Covers finding and configuring the physical
> controllers (see [concepts: controllers](02-concepts.md)).

## What this page will cover
- The **Devices** panel and how controllers are listed (grouped by device, with outputs).
- **Scanning**: the ⌖ scan control, what it probes (WLED sweep + Art-Net ArtPoll), reading
  the live progress, and adding a found controller.
- **Adding manually** with **+ Device** and setting the IP.
- Per-device settings: **IP**, **colour order** (RGB/GRB/RGBW), **protocol** (DDP vs Art-Net),
  Art-Net **base universe**, **brightness**, output **delay**.
- **Identify** (flash a controller to locate it), **status dot** (online/offline), reboot.
- Multiple controllers; the 12-QuinLED install pattern.

_See also: [Getting started §2](03-getting-started.md), [Troubleshooting](12-troubleshooting.md)._
