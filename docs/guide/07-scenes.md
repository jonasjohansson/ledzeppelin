# Scenes

> **Status: outline — draft in progress.** LED Zeppelin's recall model (used instead of a

## What this page will cover
- What a **scene** is — a snapshot of the current look you can recall.
- Saving a scene, recalling it, and how recall behaves.
- Why scenes (snapshot-recall) replace a traditional cue list.
- Tips for an installation: a handful of named looks to switch between.
