# The canvas: sources & effects

> **Status: outline — draft in progress.** How you make the visuals fixtures sample.

## What this page will cover
- The **composition** and the canvas; the single-layer + **clips** model (not a multi-layer
  VJ stack).
- Adding **sources** (generative clips, ISF shaders from the example picker).
- Adding **effects** and chaining them.
- **Parameter modulation** — timeline, audio input, external OSC/MIDI.
- The **preview** and the output-preview overlay (seeing what each fixture samples).
- How the canvas relates to fixtures (sampling) — cross-link to mapping.
